Simplicity is the way to go when considering user experience.

If the websites are confusing, visitors will give up and find what they need elsewhere. Keeping navigation simple, intuitive and consisten is key.

Visual hierarchy is the arrangement of elements in order of importance.

One of the most important functions of visual hierarchy is to establish a focal point. This shows visitors where the most important information is.

Typography has an important role to play on the website.

It commands attention and works as the visual interpretation of the brands voice.

More people are using their mobile devices to browse the web.

It is important to consider building your website with a responsive layout that can adjust to different screens.

Keep users engaged and coming back for more.
